DETAILED STRATEGIES
==================================================

HIGH RISK MONTH TO MONTH
--------------------------------------------------
1. Offer discounted annual contract (15-20% savings)
2. Provide 3-month price lock guarantee
3. Bundle services with loyalty discount
4. Offer flexible contract terms with benefits

HIGH RISK NEW CUSTOMERS
--------------------------------------------------
1. Implement onboarding follow-up program
2. Offer dedicated customer success manager
3. Provide welcome package with premium features
4. Schedule regular check-ins during first 90 days

HIGH RISK SUPPORT ISSUES
--------------------------------------------------
1. Proactive outreach to resolve outstanding issues
2. Provide premium support tier
3. Offer service credit for inconvenience
4. Assign dedicated support representative

HIGH VALUE AT RISK
--------------------------------------------------
1. VIP retention program with exclusive perks
2. Personalized account manager
3. Value-added services at no cost
4. Priority support and early access to features

GENERAL RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------
1. Competitive pricing analysis and matching
2. Enhanced loyalty rewards program
3. Regular customer satisfaction surveys
4. Personalized communication and offers
